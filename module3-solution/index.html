Chicken

Creamy Chicken

1 pound Flank Steak ¼ cup Cornstarch ¼ cup Canola Oil 2 teaspoons Fresh Ginger (minced) 1 tablespoon Garlic (minced) ⅓ cup Lite Soy Sauce (low sodium) ⅓ cup Water ½ cup Dark Brown Sugar 4 stalks Scallions green parts only, cut into 2 inch pieces

Season chicken breasts with onion powder, garlic powder, herbs, salt and pepper. Heat a large pan on medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side until they are golden and cooked through. In the same pan, add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant. Reduce the heat, and add the milk (or half and half). Stir in the cornstarch mixture into the pan, and simmer until the sauce has thickened slightly. Serve the chicken with the sauce.

Beef

Balsamic Steak Salad

Steak (your preferred cut) Mixed greens Dried cranberries Blue cheese Creamy avocado Balsamic vinegar
Instructions:
Grill or pan-sear the steak to your desired doneness. Slice the steak and arrange it on a bed of mixed greens. Top with dried cranberries, crumbled blue cheese, and avocado slices. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar.

Sushi

Ingredients:
2 nori sheets (seaweed) 1 cup of cooked rice 1 carrot (cut into thin strips) 1 cucumber (cut into thin strips) 1 avocado (cut into thin strips) ½ cup of rice vinegar 1 tablespoon of sugar Sal

Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo rolling mat. Spread a layer of cooked rice over the nori, leaving a border at the top. Arrange the carrot, cucumber, and avocado strips in the center. Roll the sushi tightly, using the bamboo mat. Slice into pieces and serve with soy sauce.
